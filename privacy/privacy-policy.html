LangChill Privacy Policy

Client

Data is only used to understand general user behavior and improve the product.
Individual users are never identifiable by us (no links to you or your account).
We never read your personal content (e.g. deck names, card contents).

We measure user behavior and retention without ever linking it to you.
User behavior is what people do in the app.
This lets us know how and what is being used.
Retention is whether people continue to use the app after a while.
This lets us know how much value people get from the app.

Measuring retention minimally requires us to use a random, persistent client ID.
It is never linked to you, your account, or any personally identifying actions or data.
It lets us know things like "Someone completed onboarding and made a card" or "80% of users continue to use the app after 1 month".

We never read the specific contents of your card decks or cards.
At most, general information like the number of cards in a deck is measured.

Properties

These are additional properties stored as part of analytics events.

  • IP-based coarse location data
    IP address is used to geolocate the city, state/province, country, then the IP is discarded.
    This lets us know in which countries users are using the app.
  • iOS and app version
  • Device details (model, screen size)
  • Connection details (carrier, radio type, using wifi)
  • Timestamp

Third-Party Providers

Their data processing guidelines state that:
• They process personal information only for the purpose of providing application services or when required by law.
• They do not disclose or provide access to personal information to any third party unless directed by us (we will not direct them to do this) or for compliance and legal reasons.

Server

Data is only used to understand general user behavior and improve the product, and to stop fraud and abuse.
Only the minimum amount of data required for this is used, and is deleted as soon as possible.

Query metadata and connection details need to be linked to an account to stop fraud and abuse.

Query metadata consists of details such as which endpoint was used, the response language, and LLM model used.
It does not include any query content.
E.g. Query metadata "User ABC used 10 queries today".
Connection details includes the IP address and client details such as the OS version and user agent.

Connection details are deleted after 30 days.
Query metadata is deleted after 90 days.

Query contents are never linked to you.
They are stored in an anonymous pile to let us understand how the app is being used, and are deleted after 60 days.
E.g. "There were 50 queries for 'hello' using GPT-4o".

Your email address, if provided, will only be used to contact you for necessary account-related matters.
If you choose the option to allow product news and special offers, then such messages will also be sent.

Users can request to have their data deleted within 10 days by submitting a request to delete their account.
This can be done by sending an email to 'support@langchill.com' from the account email, with the subject 'delete account' and the app name.

Ads

Ads support the free version of the app and free queries.
Ad data and privacy is separate from that of the application (client and server).
Ad data and privacy is managed under applicable laws such as GDPR and CPRA.
When applicable, you can manage your consent settings in "Settings / Privacy / Privacy Options".
